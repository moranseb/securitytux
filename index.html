Learn the Basics of Personal Cyber Security

Welcome to Security Tux. I hope to create straightforward guides that anyone can follow to improve their personal cyber security and stay safe online. In the guides found here, I will frequently link to to additional recources if you are interested, but my goal is to create a comprehensive starting point. While there are no iron-clad security measures, if you follow these guides you will be well on your way.

Why Listen to an Internet Stranger on Cyber Security

Working in the field of cyber security sent me down a very deep rabbit hole learning about personal cyber security and privacy. After being asked by friends and family members about how they could stay safe online, I decided to try to create a recource with plug and play guides that anyone can use to help improve their online safety. The information in these guides comes from both my professional expirience in cyber security, as well as what I've learned on my own during my time down the privacy rabit hole.
